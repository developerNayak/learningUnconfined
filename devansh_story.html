Battle in Hurtge forest

Devansh Narang Class 9

Austin and Robert were nearing the target while Philip Edwards the platoon commander was contacting the base using the wireless set to report their status the rest of the platoon was resting in their camps Philip got a transmission from Robert and Austin about their status about infiltrating the Germany ammunition dump

it was a cold December morning of 1942 the war was at its peak Austin and Robert along with the rest of the platoon were informed by the platoon commander that the general wanted to meet them regarding their next assignment at sharp 1000 hours the platoon reported to the commander at 9450 hours exact from there they were taken to the general by him.

there the general briefed them about their next mission their mission was to blow up an Nazi ammunition dump located in the Hurtge forest they would be air dropped in Chiveres Air Base Malmeldy Belgium they were then taken to their quarters underground to freshen up and prepare for their assignment in the dead of night it was 1800 hours the soldiers were armed were armed with high intensity torches to travel in fog and Austin and Robert were in charge of planting the boom the commander Philip Edwards was in charge of the wireless set witch send transmissions straight to the generals office they all had camouflaging uniforms and a couple of guns and knives each they were given a military caravan which would take then to the Belgium side of the forest they were instructed to deploy the boom at 0000 hours exact an give hourly updates even with the fog the commander was driving fast even with the fog due to which they had met a few small accidents at 2000 hours the commander stopped outside the forest the platoon got off and the commander along with some soldiers gave the emergency wireless set to platoon they were expected to give hourly updates to the commander it was 2300 hours the platoon was standing outside the dump there were a hand full of guards standing outside the soldiers covered Austin and Robert as they planted the bomb the sky was covered with bullets they quickly deployed the boom and retreated with the rest of the platoon. Uncle Robert told this story to Ralf in one of their reunions in Dusseldorf.
